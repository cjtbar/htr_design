[FAQ]

0x0A How do I join HackTheRat.org?

In order to join HackTheRat.org, you need to solve a very basic challenge that is presented here. If you are unable to "hack the register app" then I'd recommend taking a look here.

0x0C What happens if I successfully hack into HTR's website?

Your username and a quote from you will be posted publicly on the Hall of Fame along with a short story of how you hacked us. You will also be rewarded with 10,000 points and a new ranking of "HTR Admin". (Note: You will not be rewarded with actual admin privledges, just the new rank.)

0x0B I am good with computers but don't know jack about hacking... Where should I begin?

Start reading about computers, programming, operating systems, and computer security in general. Check out HTR Library where we offer a giant collection of articles, tutorials, tools, manuals and much much more, available FREE to HTR members. Then you should try and beat the challenges or try and pass the exams over at HTR Exams. The "missions" are a great tool to help you learn & practice hacking and cracking techniques.

0x0D What happens after I join HackTheRat?

After becoming a member you are presented with a private network (HTB Net) that contains several vulnerable systems. Your goal is to hack each one and after doing so, you are awarded with points that advance you to the hall of fame.
